About lilbookcorner

We are a group of students from Hanoi - Amsterdam High School for the Gifted. With the aim of spreading reading awareness and making books accessible to everyone in Hanoi, we opened an online library lilbookcorner for books lovers.

    Main activities:
  • People can borrow lilbookcorner's books during 2 weeks at certain prices.
  • The money we receive will be used to buy more books for the library.
  • To know more about borrowing process, read: How to borrow books

How to borrow books

1. Choose a book you want from each genre's book list below.

2. Contact us and send us the following information:
- Full name
- Phone number
- Book you want to borrow
- Time and place to get the book

3. Borrowing rules:
- You cannot borrow more than 2 books each time
- Deposit 50% of the printed price on the book (This money will be given back when the book is returned on time and intact)
- Maximum borrowing time: 2 weeks. If you do not return the book after 2 weeks, we will keep your deposit.

4. Price:
- Vietnamese books: 5000 VND
- Foreign-language books: 10,000 VND

Note: This money will be used for buying more books, not for personal benefits.
